About

I'm a freelance web developer. I make websites and web applications for a select group of agencies, designers and private clients.

I'm also a Y Combinator alumnus, having served as Technical Director at 80,000 Hours. There, my work received public endorsement from Steven Pinker, Peter Singer and the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation.

I hold a first class bachelors degree in philosophy and received two awards for academic achievement during my studies at the University of Sussex.

I am British but I live in Reykjavík, Iceland where I run a small coworking space by the sea.

I'm currently taking on new projects. Write to hello@peterhartree.co.uk
